# snakie

At roots this is a basic snake game that values colors over hardcore playing, a game made to pass the time when bored without being too overstimulating and too focused on scores and difficulty scaling.

It is made to look silly and not too serious while still working as a fun snake game, there are three different things you can eat, the basic fruit that gives you points, then a power up that makes you faster and invulnerable to eating yourself as well as doubling your points when eating fruits and then a power down that makes you slower and deducts your points, but if you are powered up then the power down won't affect you.
